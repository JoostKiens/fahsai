Air arrived here from the Gulf of Thailand — the modelled pm25 reads 13.4 µg/m³ at the
origin over open water, clean marine air to start. But consistent southerly winds
carried it north through the Bangkok metropolitan area before reaching this station,
picking up emissions from Bangkok (pop. 10.5M), Nonthaburi, and the Samut Prakan
industrial corridor along the way.

Fire activity has been sustained at high levels in and around this area for weeks,
150 detections locally, adding a background layer of smoke to the urban emissions
arriving from the south. The combination has kept conditions in the Unhealthy for
sensitive groups range for over a week. Nearby stations confirm the pattern: 25
stations average 44.1 µg/m³, ranging from Moderate to Unhealthy, with the reading
here consistent with the regional picture.
