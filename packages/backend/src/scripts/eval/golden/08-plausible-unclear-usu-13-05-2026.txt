Air here arrived from Indonesia via the Strait of Malacca, with modelled PM2.5 reading
Good (10.3–11.4 µg/m³) along the entire route, no fires, no identified emission
sources, and 88 mm of rain along the way. The Moderate reading at this station is only
slightly above the modelled background, and there is no clear single cause to point to.

Humidity reached 86% earlier in the week, which can cause optical sensors to over-read,
and may partly explain why the station reads somewhat above the regional model. With no
nearby reference stations, it is difficult to confirm whether this reflects actual
conditions or a lingering sensor effect from the recent heavy rainfall.
